# Privacy Policy – Doc2Notes

**Last Updated:** December 19, 2025

Doc2Notes ("the App") is a study assistant that transforms user-provided text into structured notes, key takeaways, and self-test questions. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect

### Categories of Personal Data
- **User-Provided Content:** Text content you submit for processing into study notes.

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- No account information or credentials
- No location data (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
- No device identifiers or fingerprints
- No cookies or tracking technologies
- No behavioral or usage analytics
- No conversation history beyond the current request

## How We Use Your Data

### Purpose of Processing
Your text is processed solely to:
1. Generate structured study notes with headings
2. Extract key takeaways
3. Create self-test questions

### Processing Method
- All processing occurs in real-time via OpenAI's API
- Text is sent to OpenAI for AI-powered summarization
- No data is stored on our servers after the response is returned

## Data Sharing and Recipients

| Recipient | Purpose | Data Shared |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| OpenAI | AI processing | User-submitted text |

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

## Data Retention

- **Our Servers:** Zero retention. Text is processed and discarded immediately.
- **OpenAI:** Subject to [OpenAI's data usage policies](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy). We use the API with `store: false` where supported.

## Your Rights and Controls

You have the right to:
- **Disconnect:** Remove the App from ChatGPT at any time via Settings → Apps
- **Not Use:** Simply do not submit text you don't want processed
- **Request Information:** Contact us for questions about data handling

## Security

- All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- No persistent storage means no data breach risk from our infrastructure
- We follow security best practices for API development

## Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for users aged 13 and older as part of ChatGPT. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
